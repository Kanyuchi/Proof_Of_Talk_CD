## Appendix — Easing Reference

easeOutCubic — starts fast, decelerates. Standard reveal motion.

easeInOutCubic — slow start, fast middle, slow end. Smooth transitions.

easeOutBack — overshoots slightly past the target then settles back (spring effect).

easeInCubic — starts slow, accelerates out. Used for exit motion.

useFeatureTransition — custom hook: left panel fades in/out, right panel slides vertically (1080px→0→-1080px).
