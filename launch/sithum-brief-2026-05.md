## 1. Problem

Web3 conferences are an 18-hour blur of business cards and *"let's connect on LinkedIn."*

2,500 decision-makers in one room over two days. You leave with a stack of contacts and zero real conversations. The people you actually needed to meet — the LP who would have invested, the founder who fits your thesis, the regulator who clears your path — they were there. You just never found them.

Filters and search don't fix it. You don't know who you're looking for until after you've met them.

## 2. Product Intro

**POT Matchmaker** — the AI built into Proof of Talk 2026.

It reads every registered attendee's profile, infers what each person actually needs from the conference, and produces a personalised shortlist of the 5–10 people worth your time. It's not a generic event app bolted on. It was built for this conference, for this audience.

$18 trillion in assets. 2,500 decision-makers. One AI concierge.

## 3. Use Cases / Features

*(maps to the screenshots already with Sithum)*

| Feature | What it does in the video |
|---|---|
| **My Matches** | Ranked list of who to meet, with a one-line reason for each. Not "you both work in DeFi" — actual complementarity (*"She raises funds from the LPs you're trying to reach."*) |
| **AI Concierge** | Chat with it like a smart colleague. Ask *"Who should I meet?"* or *"Prep me for my meeting with X."* It answers in plain English, sources cited. |
| **Profile auto-drafting** | Profile incomplete? The AI offers to draft your conference goals, your target companies, your interests — based on what it already knows about you. One tap and your profile is sharper, your matches improve. |
| **Mutual match alerts** | When someone you picked picks you back, the app tells you — and surfaces the time slots you're both free. |
| **Free-slot booking** | One tap to book a meeting at a time that works for both of you. No back-and-forth. |
| **Magic link** | No password, no signup friction. Your meetings open from a single link in your email. |

## 4. Impact / Resolution

Walk out of Paris with **5 conversations that move your business forward** — not 50 contacts that go cold by Monday.

- Investors find the founders in their thesis.
- Founders find the LPs who'd actually invest.
- Regulators find the builders they need to talk to.
- Sponsors find the buyers they came to meet.

No keyword filters. No scrolling through 2,500 profiles. The AI did the reading for you. You just show up.
